## Features

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Weekly View" icon="sun">
    7-day columns showing your week at a glance. Navigate with `h/j/k/l`, drag
    items between days.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Backlog View" icon="list-format">
    4-column organizer for "someday" items. Press `b` to toggle, organize ideas
    without cluttering your week.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Workspaces & Projects" icon="open-book">
    Group todos into workspaces and projects. Manage from CLI or assign via
    `mach add -p myproject`.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Vim Navigation" icon="rocket">
    `h/j/k/l` to move, `Enter` to select and drag, `dd` to delete. Feels like
    home for terminal users.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Local-First" icon="laptop">
    SQLite storage, no cloud, no account needed. Your data stays on your
    machine.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
